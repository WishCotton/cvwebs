Portfolio Of Visual Works

These are some examples of work I've done through pradominatly creative coding and some 3D modeling. I began my interest in creative coding through working with processing in community college back in the states. A big part of why I chose to take the media design programe at vic was because I would be able to continue studying creative coding. While at vic I furthered my knowledge of creative coding through P5JS which helped segway me into interest in HTML and CSS. I also started combining some of my coded work with image editing and exploring 3D modelling.

This is a video of a program I wrote to draw a random composition based on specific constraints. There are multiple different lines being drawn in different colors that move in different was. The result is a composition that is different every time but with a similar overarching look.
